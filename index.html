Who am I?

I am lucy. some time ago, I was interested in video editing, but my computer was so bad at the time that I thought trimming videos would be enough. Of course, those days are over now, and I still edit videos to this day. My goal is to earn at least a small income from videos, enough to make a living.

I am 15 years old and really enjoy games, anime/manga, and books (Oyasumi Punpun is my favorite manga). I first encountered a computer at the age of 3, and I've been hooked ever since. Around 9 or 10, I discovered a game called "Counter-Strike" and I still play it to this day.

I currently live in Belgium and I am fluent in English, Dutch, French, and Turkish. I’m also learning Japanese. As for programming languages, I know some basic C/C++ and I also know Python & JavaScript. I wanted to create my own OS, just like Terry A. Davis. I used to be very passionate about computers in general, but I’m not as knowledgeable or enthusiastic as I used to be. This website was also coded by me. I use a simple DWM setup on Gentoo, and on my laptop, I use Void Linux with Window Maker.
